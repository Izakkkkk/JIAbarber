Garibaldi

The Garibaldi beard style is a full, thick beard with a rounded bottom and a mustache that is connected to the beard. This style is named after an Italian revolutionary, Giuseppe Garibaldi, who was known for sporting a similar look. It requires some maintenance to keep the beard looking neat and tidy. Regular trimming and shaping of the beard is necessary to keep it from getting too wild and unruly. This beard style works best for men with a square or oval face shape and can be adapted to different lengths depending on personal preference. The Garibaldi beard is a popular choice for men who want a rugged and masculine look that is both stylish and sophisticated.

Hair Cut

01-Jan-2045

Classic Pompadour

The Classic Pompadour is a timeless men's haircut that has been around for decades. It features short sides and back with longer hair on top that is styled up and back, creating a dramatic, high-volume look. The length on top can vary, but typically ranges from 3 to 5 inches. The hair is styled using pomade or wax to create a sleek, polished finish. This haircut is perfect for those who want a stylish and sophisticated look that is versatile enough to be worn in a variety of settings, from the office to a night out on the town. It is also great for those with round or square face shapes, as the height on top creates the illusion of a longer face.

Beard Style

01-Jan-2045

The Van Dyke

The Van Dyke is a classic beard style that features a short, pointed beard and a mustache. The beard is trimmed to a point, while the mustache is usually kept thin and styled in a way that complements the beard. This style is versatile and can be worn in a variety of lengths and shapes, making it a popular choice among men who want a stylish yet low-maintenance look. The Van Dyke beard is perfect for men with a square or round face shape, as it helps to elongate the face and add definition to the jawline.
